# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to or uses LazyTerra !

## Our Standards

We expect all community members to:

- **Be respectful** — treat others with kindness and consideration
- **Communicate constructively** — provide helpful feedback and be open to receiving it
- **Be collaborative** — work together towards making LazyTerra better
- **Be professional** — keep discussions focused on the project and technical matters

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions) and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from community best practices with a focus on respectful communication and collaboration.
